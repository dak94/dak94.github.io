Motyf

Motyf is a typographic festival celebrating and displaying work that will push the medium of typography forward, the title of this years (2018) event is "Virtuality: Typography in Cyberspace". The event is high tech so I wanted that to be reflected in the space through the use of generative art/typography and projection to create an impactful and forward looking design for the event.

Lucid

Lucid is a smart alarm that is aimed at allowing users to access a dataset centered around how they sleep, giving them concrete information on what helps them (individually) sleep by collecting data during the night and therefore giving them valuable information to base decisions on their sleeping habits on.

The Thinking house

The Thinking House at Bord Bia specialize in bringing Irish food brands to success in international markets through data driven strategy and design. It gave me a great excuse to play with particle systems and noise images to represent data for the window display they wanted for the project.
