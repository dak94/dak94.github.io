    Echo was my grad show project which was concerned with our level of autonomy online and the sometimes opaque mechanisms in our digital infrastructure that influence it.

    Consisting of a website and associated social media campaign, the aim of the project was to be a library of the best and most insightful minds talking or writing about how our use of the internet can change the way we see the world and influence our behaviour.

    To suit the tone and subject of the project I chose to use a visual language of generative art in the form of wireframed type animation with semi-random binary code patterns.

    The core of this identity is the 3d wire-framed text, It makes.Advertisement and wayfinding were a large part of this project, so finding and exploiting different methods of public advertisement was essential, from the building masthead to digital signs in the city.
