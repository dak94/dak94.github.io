36 Days of Type

This is a collection of my submissions to 36 days of type 2023. I really enjoy the event and I think that by practicing design in more experimental than typical contexts it's possible to grow a more expansive conceptual repertoire in professional practice.

C

Instancing is a powerful technique for generating graphics on each point of an input geometry. In this case I used a grid to generate ellipses and used a looping perlin noise field to assign their sizes. Made in Touchdesigner and finished in After Effects.

A

One of the more complex renders in the project. This was made by modelling and animating the rotating A in Blender and then using Touchdesigner to re-render. Thickness of the lines was modulated by pixel data from the original render. The difficulty in the final rendering was in displacing the original render by the inverse of the curve of the lines to provide an undistorted letterform.

poster designvideo artmotion design3D

D

Made solely in After Effects this was a relatively simple idea. Blurring and resharpening ellipses (through levelling) gives a blobby pseudo metaball effect.

E

I've always enjoyed mixing historical and contemporary styles so I was glad of the opportunity to animate this Gothic glyph into a 3d faux neon shape.

N

This letter used a square bent along bezier curves to create a repeating N inspired by the Nintendo logo.

Motyf

A brief given to us during my B.A. by the organisers of Motyf. Motyf is a typographic festival celebrating and displaying work that will push the medium of typography forward, the title of this years (2018) event is "Virtuality: Typography in Cyberspace".
