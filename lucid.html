    Lucid is a smart alarm app that allows you to monitor your sleep through a process of gamification. Gamification is a great way to boost engagement with an app by making a usually mundane task more interesting through dramatisation, competition and in app awards/achievements.

    Smart alarms make waking up a lot easier by detecting when you are in a light sleep close to your usual alarm time and gently pushing you into lucidity, rather than blasting you out of deep sleep every morning.


    The main aspect of gamification in this project is the personalised sleep infographic. With each coloured block representing a nights sleep, the aim is to keep your infographic as green as possible and with long nights of sleep.


    A fairly conventional alarm interface is the keystone of the project and the first thing a user sees when they open the app.

    The sleep infographic is one swipe away, letting users quickly access a visual compendium of their last few weeks sleep quality.


    The filter is likely the most powerful component of the app, giving users a tool to analyse their sleep patterns and how they relate to their daily activities turns the infographic from a fun game into an actionable tool for improving your sleep quality.

    Connecting to social media and other health or fitness apps helps integrate Lucid into an ecosystem of apps that are more effective when used in conjunction, not running as fast as you'd like? You might be faster with a better nights sleep.
