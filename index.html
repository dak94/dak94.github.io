D&AD Penguin Youth

This project is a brief from the popular student design competition run by D&AD. The brief was to re-imagine and revitalise the Penguin Youth brand. I chose to use motion graphics as a way to bring short snippets of content from Penguin Youth books onto short form content platforms like Snapchat and Tiktok that are popular with young adults.
